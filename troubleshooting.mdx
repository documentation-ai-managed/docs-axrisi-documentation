## Common Issues

Most problems with Axrisi can be resolved with simple troubleshooting steps.

<Expandable title="Extension Not Appearing" default-open="false">
  Ensure the extension is enabled in your browser settings and pinned to the toolbar.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Transformations Failing" default-open="false">
  Check your internet connection and verify you're not exceeding free plan limits.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Slow Performance" default-open="false">
  Clear your browser cache and ensure you have sufficient RAM available.
</Expandable>

## Browser-Specific Issues

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Chrome" icon="chrome">
    Check extensions page and ensure Axrisi has necessary permissions.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Firefox" icon="firefox">
    Verify the add-on is not disabled in private browsing mode.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Edge" icon="edge">
    Update to the latest version for compatibility.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
