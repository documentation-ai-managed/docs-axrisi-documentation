## Detailed Feature Breakdown

### Summarization

The summarization feature reduces lengthy articles or documents to their essential points while maintaining key information.

<Callout kind="tip">Ideal for news articles, research papers, and lengthy blog posts.</Callout>

### Bulletization

Transform dense paragraphs into structured bullet points for better readability and quick scanning.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Before" icon="x-circle">
    Original paragraph format with long sentences.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="After" icon="check-circle">
    Clean bullet points for easy consumption.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Explanation

Break down technical jargon or complex ideas into simple, understandable language.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Text" icon="mouse-pointer">
    Highlight the complex text you want explained.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Explain" icon="help-circle">
    Right-click and select the explanation option.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Output" icon="eye">
    Read the simplified explanation in place.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Continuation

Generate logical continuations for incomplete thoughts or truncated content.

<Expandable title="How Continuation Works" default-open="false">
  The AI analyzes the context and tone of the selected text to provide natural extensions.
</Expandable>

### Correction

Fix grammatical errors, improve sentence structure, and enhance overall writing quality.

<CodeGroup tabs="Grammar,Style">
  ```javascript
const original = "The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.";
const corrected = "The quick brown fox jumped over the lazy dog.";
console.log(corrected);
  ```
  ```javascript
const improved = "This sentence has been enhanced for better clarity and flow.";
console.log(improved);
  ```
</CodeGroup>
